# 🚀 Jugaad GPT  
Gemini-powered AI repair copilot for broken appliances, real-time vision guidance, and low-cost jugaad fixes from locally available parts. 🔧📸🧠

---

## 🧠 Overview  
Jugaad GPT is a multimodal repair assistant that analyzes broken appliances from photos, video, or live camera input, identifies likely faults, and generates step-by-step repair guidance with wiring help, assembly instructions, rough sketches, measurements, and budget-friendly kirana-store part suggestions.

It behaves like a **real-time AI repair copilot** with:
- Step-by-step guided workflows  
- Visual verification of repairs  
- Live correction feedback  
- Voice-enabled assistance  
- A premium, dashboard-based experience  

---

## 😵‍💫 Problem  
Broken appliances are hard to fix because:
- People don’t know what exactly failed  
- Repair information is scattered and technical  
- Service repairs are expensive and slow  
- Buying replacement items is often unnecessary  

Simple problems feel complex and costly.

---

## 💡 Solution  
Jugaad GPT uses Gemini vision + reasoning to:
- Detect issues from images or live camera feed  
- Explain problems in simple language  
- Generate a structured repair plan  
- Guide users step-by-step  
- Verify each step before continuing  
- Correct mistakes instantly  

It turns repair into a **guided, interactive process** instead of guesswork.

---

## 🛠️ What It Does  

- 📸 Diagnoses appliances from images, video, or live camera  
- 🎙️ Voice mode for hands-free repair guidance  
- 🧩 One-step-at-a-time repair instructions  
- ✅ Step verification before moving forward  
- ⚠️ Detects mistakes and gives instant fixes  
- 🛒 Suggests low-cost kirana-store parts and substitutes

  - Safety checks

  - Analytics
